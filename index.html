About Me

Websites are pretty neat. I feel like the goal of all young coders is to build one. The sterotype of asking programmers to build an app/website further plays into this. I've been coding for I'd say long enough (nearly senior in college now) so I figured I'd start with making a static site with github pages. This quick writeup is mostly for something to fill the homepage if I'm being honest, but I hope I can expand both this website and associated github projects. Like everyone I have quite a few ideas for projects, we will see if I get around to any of them at this point.

Interest Areas

Web Development

During the 2020 pandemic, I learned a basic dev stack with HTML+CSS+JS and Flask. I recently started learning a MERN stack, and am actively improving my abilities.

Digital Twins

Digital twins are virtual models designed to reflect the state of a physical object. I was introduced to it as an intern at CACI and loved the blend of mechanical engineering and computer science. Very much an interest for now, but plenty of learning about IoT and networking is to come.

Tunnel Boring

While at Virginia Tech, I had the pleasure to work with "The Diggeridoos", a tunnel boring competition team. Designing and building a tunnel boring machine as a small (mostly) undergraduate team was difficult, but proved a well rounded engineering problem requiring software, sensor, power system, control system, structural, and hydraulic knowledge.
